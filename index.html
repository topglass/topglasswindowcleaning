About Us

Top Glass Window Cleaners are a family run commercial window cleaning company based in Ilkeston covering Nottingham, Derby and the surrounding areas. Established in 2005, we have gained an ever expanding client base specialising in large scale commercial properties. Here at Top Glass we strive for perfection in our work with an eye for detail like no other, ensuring the customer is left with ultimate satisfaction after each and every clean. All our staff are uniformed, professional, health & safety trained and fully insured, leaving each and every job to a consistent high standard that cannot be matched. We're proud of what we do.

Services We Offer

Commercial Window Cleaning

Top Glass Window Cleaners are an experienced, professional commercial window cleaning company having spent the last 17 years growing our ever expanding commercial client base including schools, offices, factories, car showrooms and hospitality venues. We equip our operatives with the latest state of the art waterfed pole technology, reaching up to heights of 65ft whilst keeping our feet safely on the ground enables us to ensure any task can be completed with ultimate ease, providing a perfect finish on every visit... garunteed. We produce our own purified water so that we can keep our prices competitive and garuntee only the best product is used to clean our customers Windows.
